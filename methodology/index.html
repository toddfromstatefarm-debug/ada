Methodology

The calculator uses a simple equation: (hours saved per week × hourly rate × 4) - monthly price.

What matters most

  • Your hourly rate or time value
  • How often you actually use the tool
  • Whether the time savings estimate is realistic
  • The current monthly price of the software

How to read the result

  • Strong positive result: often worth it
  • Small positive result: borderline, depends on use
  • Negative result: probably not worth it yet

Prices and features change. Always verify current pricing before making a final decision.
