Otter.ai Review

Otter.ai is easy to justify when meetings create real note-taking or follow-up pain.

Best for

Client-facing professionals, students, and teams that want searchable transcripts instead of relying on memory or scattered notes.

Not ideal for

People with very few meetings or workflows where recordings and transcripts are not acceptable.

Key features

  • Transcribes meetings automatically
  • Creates searchable notes and summaries
  • Makes follow-up details easier to recover later

Pros

  • Strong everyday fit for frequent meetings
  • Useful for people who miss details during live calls
  • Searchability can save hours over time

Cons

  • Less valuable if you rarely meet
  • Transcript quality still depends on audio quality
  • Some users will have privacy concerns with meeting capture
