Quick Verdict

Todoist AI is the better fit for people who want a lightweight daily task system with some AI assistance. Sunsama is better for users who want a more intentional daily planning ritual tied closely to calendars and workload balancing.

Best For

Choose Todoist AI for simplicity and lightweight task management. Choose Sunsama for structured daily planning and a calmer end-to-end workday workflow.

When to Choose One Over the Other

Pick Todoist AI if you want quick capture and prioritization without much overhead. Pick Sunsama if you want to actively shape each day around realistic workloads and calendar constraints.
