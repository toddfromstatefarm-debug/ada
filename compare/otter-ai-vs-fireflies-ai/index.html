Quick Verdict

Otter.ai is usually the cleaner pick for solo professionals, students, and individual note-takers. Fireflies.ai becomes more compelling when a team needs shared notes, action items, and CRM-friendly meeting workflows.

Best For

Choose Otter.ai for personal note capture and searchable transcripts. Choose Fireflies.ai for team collaboration and shared meeting intelligence.

When to Choose One Over the Other

Pick Otter.ai if you mainly want reliable transcription and easy note search. Pick Fireflies.ai if your team wants meeting summaries distributed across tools and workflows.
