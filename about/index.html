About This Site

AI Productivity Calculators helps freelancers, solo operators, and small teams decide whether software tools actually save enough time to justify the monthly cost.

The site is built around one practical question: does the tool pay for itself in your real workflow? The calculators are decision aids, not guarantees.

The goal is simple: cut through hype, make the math visible, and help users make better software decisions.
